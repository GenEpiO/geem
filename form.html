This form viewer is tested with Google Chrome v. 62; other browsers will be supported in the future. It may take time to load.

This GEEM form viewer can render the HTML form for a given ontology identifier (assuming it has been annotated as a GEEM specification). Simply include the identifier of the desired form, for example: http://genepio.org/geem/form.html#GENEPIO:0001624


Minimize form view

When minimize form is activated, only required fields or fields with selections will show their input elements. All other fields will be shown by label only. Mousing over a minimized field label will open it to its full size for data entry.

The specification used to make this form is available in a variety of formats.
